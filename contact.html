Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide! International shipping rates vary by location and package weight. Please contact us for a quote to your specific country.

How do I know if a vintage camera is in good condition?

All our cameras undergo thorough testing and inspection. We provide detailed condition reports and photos for each item. Our grading system ranges from Mint (like new) to Used (fully functional with signs of wear).

Do you provide warranties on vintage cameras?

Yes, we offer a 30-day warranty on all mechanical and electronic functions of our cameras. This doesn't cover cosmetic wear but ensures your camera will work perfectly when you receive it.

Can I trade in my old camera?

Absolutely! We offer trade-in programs for vintage cameras. Bring your camera to our store or send us photos and details for an evaluation. We'll give you a fair trade-in value toward your purchase.

Do you offer photography lessons?

Yes! We offer regular workshops and one-on-one lessons covering everything from basic film photography to advanced darkroom techniques. Check our services page for current offerings.
